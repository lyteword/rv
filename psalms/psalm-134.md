# Psalm 134 

**¹** A Song of Ascents. Behold, bless ye the LORD, all ye servants of the LORD, which by night stand in the house of the LORD. 

**²** Lift up your hands to the sanctuary, and bless ye the LORD. 

**³** The LORD bless thee out of Zion; even he that made heaven and earth.
